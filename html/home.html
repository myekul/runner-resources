OBS

Need a solid screen recorder or streaming application to capture your gameplay? Open Broadcaster Software (OBS) is an excellent option, used by the vast majority of runners.

LiveSplit

The golden standard speedrun timer.

Once installed, you can navigate to split settings and change the game to Cuphead to enable the load remover. You can also configure the autosplitter to automatically split upon defeating a boss.

Debug Mod

Do you want an HP bar? How about a level selector? Ever wanted to manipulate RNG? The debug mod can do all that and more.

Download instructions are located at the bottom of the GitHub page.
